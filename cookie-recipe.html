Chocolate Chip Cookies

  1. Home
  2. Cookie Recipe

Do This

  1. Preheat oven 200c
  2. Add flour, baking soda in small bowl and beat butter with granulated sugar and vanilla extract in a large mixer bowl until creamy
  3. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Gradually beat in flour mixture. Stir in chopped choclolate
  4. Cool well the batter, at least for 2 hours.
  5. Roll the dough into small balls and place on baking paper
  6. Bake the cookies for 10mins or until golden brown. Let the cookies cooldown on tray

Ingredients

The recipe is valid for pcs ( )

  • cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) butter
  • cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • large eggs
  • 1 cup chopped choclolate
